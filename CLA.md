## How to sign

Signing is handled automatically: the first time You open a pull
request against the Project, the [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io/)
bot will comment with a one-click sign-in link. The signature is bound
to Your GitHub account and covers all Your future contributions to the
Project — You only sign once.

If You prefer not to use CLA Assistant, You may instead include the
following line in the body of Your pull request:

— signed with the same name and email associated with Your commits.
